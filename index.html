Odd / Even

Pick a number (0–5), choose Even or Odd — sum decides the winner.

Press Enter to start

Game Rules

  1. Player picks a number from 0 to 5.
  2. Player chooses Even or Odd.
  3. The computer randomly picks a number (0–5).
  4. We add both numbers. If the sum matches your Even/Odd choice, you win the round.
  5. First to reach the target score (default 3 or 5 or 7) wins the game.
