Even / Odd Game

Pick a number, choose Even or Odd, and try to beat the computer!

Rules

1. Pick a number from 0 to 5.

2. Choose Even or Odd.

3. The computer also picks a random number from 0 to 5.

4. Both numbers are added together.

If the sum matches your choice (Even or Odd), you get a point. Otherwise, the computer gets a point.

First to reach 5 points wins.
